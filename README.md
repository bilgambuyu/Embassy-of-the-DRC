## Overview

This is the **official diplomatic website** for the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) to the United Nations Office at Geneva and other international organizations in Switzerland.

The website serves as a **digital gateway** for:
- Swiss and international citizens seeking consular services
- Diplomats and international organizations engaging with the Mission
- Investors and businesses exploring opportunities in the DRC
- The Congolese diaspora in Switzerland and neighboring countries

### Objectives

| Objective | Implementation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Information Dissemination** | Clear presentation of consular services, visa requirements, and diplomatic activities |
| **National Branding** | Showcase DRC's cultural heritage, natural resources, and investment potential |
| **Accessibility** | Multilingual-ready structure and mobile-first responsive design |
| **Credibility** | Professional diplomatic aesthetic aligned with governmental standards |

## Features

### Core Functionality

- ** Homepage with Hero Section**
  - Full-width banner featuring DRC imagery
  - Mission statement and welcome message from the Ambassador
  - Quick-access cards for key services

- ** Consular Services Portal**
  - Visa application guidelines and requirements
  - Passport renewal procedures
  - Civil status documentation (birth, marriage, death certificates)
  - Fee structure and processing times

- **🇨🇩 About the DRC**
  - Geography and demographics
  - History and political system
  - Economy and investment opportunities
  - Culture and tourism highlights

- ** News & Announcements**
  - Official communiqués from the Embassy
  - Upcoming events and diplomatic engagements
  - Press releases and media gallery

- ** Contact & Location**
  - Interactive Google Maps integration
  - Embassy address and office hours
  - Emergency contact numbers for Congolese citizens
  - Contact form with email validation

- ** Bilateral Relations**
  - DRC-Switzerland diplomatic history
  - Cooperation with UN agencies in Geneva
  - Trade and development partnerships

### Technical Features

- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Fast Loading**: Static site generation with optimized assets
- **SEO Ready**: Semantic HTML structure and meta tags
- **Multilingual Foundation**: Structure prepared for French/English toggle
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliant color contrast and keyboard navigation
- **Security**: HTTPS enforced via Netlify, form spam protection
